Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Information We Collect

PDFNest is a browser-based PDF tool. All file processing happens locally in your browser. We do not upload, store, or transmit your files to any server.

When you use our service, we may collect:

  • Basic analytics data (pages visited, tool usage patterns)
  • Device and browser information for service improvement
  • Cookies for website functionality (see Cookie Policy below)

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain our website
  • Understand how visitors use our tools
  • Improve user experience and service quality
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

3. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze site traffic and usage
  • Serve personalized advertisements via third-party ad networks

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — for website analytics
  • Google AdSense — for displaying advertisements
  • Vercel — for website hosting and infrastructure

These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

Since all file processing occurs in your browser, your files are never transmitted to our servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect our website and user data.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of certain data collection

To exercise these rights, please contact us through our contact page.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
